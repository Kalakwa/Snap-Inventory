How it works

This is an Inventory tracking website to be used by Snapology, Minnapolis location staff. Below you will enter the number of ounces, gallons, bags, or individual objects into the box provided. You will then enter any special comments for items needed or other circumstances you wish to provide awareness to. Click the submit button to send the email to Aaron Hagebak and update the Inventory list.
